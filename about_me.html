Jenna Bean - MSCS Student, Tufts University

My UK trip:

In December 2024 I visited the UK! My fiance grew up just outside of London and his parents still live there, so we visited for Christmas.
It was my first time leaving the US. I had a lot of fun exploring London and seeing The National Gallery, Shakespeare's Globe, Big Ben, and visiting Christmas markets!
I also met my fiance's parents for the first time. It was a really great trip!

My favorite restaurant:

New Thailand Cuisine, Lebanon, NH

New Thailand Cuisine has not only great food, but an amazing atmosphere. My favorite meal to get is their red curry with chicken.
The decor inside the restaurant is super cute, I love all of the plants and the very rustic wood tables. The staff is always lovely.

My favorite food:

My favorite food is feta pasta, A.K.A TikTok pasta. It is made with baked feta and grape tomatoes, red pepper flakes, thyme, basil, and farfalle pasta.
I love this pasta so much that I eat it on its own as an entire meal, no other sides or veggies. I could eat this for every meal for the rest of my life and die happy.
